About WanderWallet

WanderWallet is a smart AI-powered travel and budget binder designed to help users plan trips, track expenses, and save efficiently — all in one dashboard.

Developed by Shruti Singh Vishene, Harshita Mundra, Shemani Taneja, and Priyanshu Soni, this project aims to make financial planning and travel management simple, intelligent, and user-friendly.
